Welcome to SD Residency

SD Residency is a hotel located in the scenic hill station of Yercaud Town, Tamil Nadu. The hotel offers comfortable rooms, delicious cuisine, and a range of amenities to make your stay memorable. Whether you are looking for a relaxing getaway, a family vacation, or a business trip, sd Residency can cater to your needs. You can enjoy the panoramic views of the surrounding hills, valleys, and lakes from your balcony, or explore the nearby attractions such as the botanical garden, the rose garden, and the pagoda point. sd Residency is also conveniently located near the bus stand and the railway station, making it easy to reach from any part of the country. Book your stay today and experience the hospitality of sd Residency.

Our facilities mainly include free Wi-Fi, Travel Desk, Safety deposit boxes, Full time generator Backup, Room heater, 24 hours Hot water and Doctor on call. All the business class customers or the holiday travelers enjoy the stay at our residency. We have executive rooms for single persons to family room for four people with all the most modern amenities which will make your stay more comfortable.
